Campus Brews: 4 Unique Drinks at CCET(DG)

Written by: Viren Passi

Last Updated: August 23, 2024

1. Iced Matcha at The Green Cafe

Location: Near Block B, CCET(DG), Sector 26

The Green Cafe offers a refreshing Iced Matcha, a perfect blend of earthy matcha and chilled milk. It's a great pick-me-up, especially on warm days when you need something cool and energizing.

Recommendation: Try the Iced Matcha with almond milk for a nutty twist!

2. Cold Brew Coffee at BrewLab

Location: Near Block C, CCET(DG), Sector 26

BrewLab is known for its expertly crafted Cold Brew Coffee. Steeped over 12 hours, this drink is smooth and less acidic than your average iced coffee. Perfect for a caffeine kick during study sessions.

Recommendation: Add a splash of vanilla syrup for a hint of sweetness!

3. Fresh Lemonade at Citrus Corner

Location: Near Block D, CCET(DG), Sector 26

Citrus Corner offers a zesty Fresh Lemonade that's just the right mix of tangy and sweet. It's an invigorating option to keep you hydrated between classes.

Recommendation: Try the Mint Lemonade for an extra refreshing experience!

4. Smoothie Bowl at Health Hub

Location: Near Block E, CCET(DG), Sector 26

Health Hub offers a delightful Smoothie Bowl, packed with fresh fruits, nuts, and seeds. It's a wholesome and satisfying option for those who want a nutritious and delicious treat.

Recommendation: Try the Berry Blast Smoothie Bowl with extra granola for crunch!
